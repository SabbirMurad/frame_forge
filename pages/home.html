Everything from canvas to code

One tool for the whole front end — design it, structure your data, and ship the code.

Visual canvas

Frames, containers, text and images with auto-layout — row, column, wrap or stack. Drag, snap, align and nest.

Design tokens

Reusable color variables and typography styles, defined once and applied everywhere across your design.

Models & mock data

Define typed data models and enums, then generate realistic mock instances to design against real-shaped data.

Flutter code export

Turn your models and providers into clean, idiomatic Dart files with a single click — ready to drop into your app.
